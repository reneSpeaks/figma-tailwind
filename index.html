Our Story

Nestled in the heart of nature, our hotel has a rich history dating back to the early 1900s. From its humble beginnings, it has grown into a luxurious retreat where guests can experience comfort, elegance, and tranquility. Our commitment to hospitality has made us a timeless destination for those seeking the perfect escape.

Fine Dining

Our fine dining restaurant offers an exceptional culinary experience. From locally sourced ingredients to expertly crafted dishes, each meal is a celebration of flavor and creativity. Enjoy your meal in an intimate setting with views that enhance the gourmet experience.

Our Spa

Rejuvenate your mind, body, and soul at our award-winning spa. Step into a world of tranquility where luxurious treatments meet natural serenity. Whether you're enjoying a calming massage, an invigorating facial, or a peaceful soak in our heated outdoor pool, our spa is designed to restore balance and harmony.

Outdoor Activities

Our outdoor activities are designed to bring you closer to nature. Whether you're hiking in the mountains, enjoying a campfire under the stars, or simply taking a stroll through our scenic grounds, you'll find plenty of ways to connect with the great outdoors.

Enjoy and join the handful of guests who already sent their best photographic memories of their stay.
